# AI-102: Azure AI Engineer Associate

> **⚠️ This track is under construction.** Challenges coming soon.

## Exam Overview

- **Exam:** AI-102 (Redesigned April 2025)
- **Level:** Associate
- **Target Audience:** AI Engineers, Solution Architects
- **Cost:** $165 USD
- **Duration:** 100 minutes
- **Passing Score:** 700/1000
- **Validity:** 1 year (renewal required)

## Why This Exam Matters for AI Architects

AI-102 was significantly redesigned in April 2025 to include **agentic solutions** and **generative AI** as major topics, reflecting the shift to Azure AI Foundry. It's now the most directly relevant certification for solution architects building AI on Azure.

## Skills Measured (April 2025)

| Domain | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Plan and manage Azure AI solutions | 20-25% |
| Implement generative AI solutions | 15-20% |
| Implement agentic AI solutions | 5-10% |
