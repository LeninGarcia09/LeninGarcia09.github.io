# Phase 1: Executive AI Foundation

## Outcomes

1. Build practical AI literacy for executive decisions
2. Understand how AI changes enterprise growth models in industrial markets
3. Define personal AI operating model for business development leadership

## Learning Modules

- AI strategy fundamentals for non-technical executives
- GenAI and agentic systems: what matters for commercial leaders
- Data readiness, architecture basics, and enterprise constraints
- AI economics: ROI, productivity, and scaling costs
- Responsible AI and risk fundamentals (NIST/EU AI Act concepts)

## Practical Work

- Map top growth levers where AI can produce measurable impact
- Build an executive AI glossary for cross-functional communication
- Create first AI initiative shortlist with value and risk assumptions

## Recommended Training

- **MIT Sloan — Artificial Intelligence: Implications for Business Strategy** (non-technical, executive AI roadmap)
- **Wharton — AI Strategy and Decision Making** (extends his Wharton CSO credential)
- **Kellogg — AI Strategies for Business Transformation** (industry/vertical AI application)
- Case studies focused on **mining and industrial B2B** commercialization

All programs are verified with primary-source links in [Sources & Verification](../sources-and-verification.md).
