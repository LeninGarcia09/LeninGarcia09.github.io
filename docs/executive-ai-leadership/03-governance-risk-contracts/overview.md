# Phase 3: Governance, Risk, and Contracts

## Outcomes

1. Build commercially usable AI governance aligned to enterprise risk posture
2. Integrate AI/data/IP risk controls into negotiation workflows
3. Strengthen cross-jurisdiction decision quality for complex agreements

## Learning Modules

- Responsible AI controls for enterprise commercialization
- Data governance and information handling for customer-facing AI usage
- AI contract terms: liability, IP, confidentiality, auditability, model outputs
- Jurisdictional risk patterns across North America, LATAM, and Europe
- Operating model for legal/commercial/technical escalation

## Practical Work

- Create AI negotiation clause library for enterprise deals
- Define escalation matrix for governance exceptions
- Implement policy for approved commercial AI use cases

## Target Business Effects

- Reduced legal and reputational exposure
- Faster alignment between business development and legal
- Stronger trust with strategic customers and executive stakeholders
