# Practical, High-Value Projects

Each project is designed to create direct commercial value and executive visibility.

## 1. AI-Enhanced Strategic Account Playbook

**Business objective:** Increase penetration and win probability in Tier-1 global accounts.

**What to build:**

- Account intelligence template (stakeholders, business priorities, risk posture)
- AI-generated account hypotheses with human validation
- Executive map for decision makers and influence strategy

**Value signal:** Better qualification quality and stronger executive engagement plans.

## 2. Global Opportunity Reactivation Engine

**Business objective:** Systematically revive dormant opportunities.

**What to build:**

- AI workflow for opportunity triage and reactivation prioritization
- Region-specific messaging strategy (English/Spanish)
- Playbooks for re-engagement timing and value narrative

**Value signal:** Reactivated opportunities entering formal discussions.

## 3. Contract Negotiation Copilot

**Business objective:** Improve speed and quality in complex contract negotiations.

**What to build:**

- Clause-risk library for industrial and technology agreements
- AI-assisted redline preparation framework
- Multi-jurisdiction issue matrix (commercial, legal, operational)

**Value signal:** Faster negotiation cycles and improved term quality.

## 4. CEO/Board AI Growth Dashboard

**Business objective:** Support high-quality growth decisions with credible metrics.

**What to build:**

- Executive KPI dashboard (pipeline, conversion, cycle time, risk)
- Narrative layer: what changed, why, and what decision is required
- Scenario view: conservative/base/aggressive growth outcomes

**Value signal:** More focused Board conversations and faster decisions.

## 5. Responsible AI Commercial Governance Framework

**Business objective:** Enable adoption without unmanaged legal, reputational, or compliance risk.

**What to build:**

- Approved use-case taxonomy for business development
- Data/PII/IP guardrails for commercial workflows
- Escalation and approval process across legal/compliance/IT

**Value signal:** Sustainable AI scale with reduced policy friction.
