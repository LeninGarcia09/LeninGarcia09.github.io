# Executive AI Learning & Development Plan

## Profile This Plan Is Built For

Global Business Development Executive and Mechanical Civil Engineer (MBA) with 20+ years leading:

- International commercial expansion across North America, Latin America, the Caribbean, and Europe
- Complex enterprise negotiations in capital-intensive industrial markets
- Executive and Board-level growth strategy decisions
- Cross-functional alignment across technical, legal, financial, and operational stakeholders

## Strategic Objective (12-18 Months)

Transform from high-performing commercial growth executive into an **AI-enabled Growth and Transformation Leader** who can:

1. Use AI to increase pipeline quality, speed, and conversion in enterprise sales cycles
2. Negotiate stronger contracts with clear AI/data/IP and risk terms
3. Drive responsible AI adoption across regions and business units
4. Lead Board-level AI growth discussions backed by measurable business value

---

## Plan Design Principles

- **Business-value first:** every learning block ties to revenue, margin, risk, or velocity
- **Executive practical:** no low-value technical depth; enough architecture fluency to make strong decisions
- **Industry-aligned:** grounded in **mining, heavy industry, energy, and industrial B2B** — his actual domain, not generic IT tracks
- **Global operating model:** multilingual (English/Spanish), cross-jurisdiction, and governance-aware

:::caution Aligned to his expertise — not to developer tracks
This plan intentionally **excludes coding certifications** (e.g., AI-102, which is a Python/C# developer exam). The training targets an **executive commercial leader**: AI strategy, commercial-AI, governance, and mining/industrial-sector applications. See [Programs & Credentials Roadmap](./certification-roadmap.md) and [Sources & Verification](./sources-and-verification.md).
:::
