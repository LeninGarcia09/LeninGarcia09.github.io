# Phase 2: Commercial AI for Growth

> **Goal:** Turn AI literacy into commercial results — faster qualification, sharper account strategy, and stronger negotiation preparation across global industrial deals.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Duration** | 8 weeks |
| **Time commitment** | 6-10 hours/week |
| **Cost** | $0 core path (paid programs optional) |
| **Capstone deliverable** | **AI-Enhanced Strategic Account Playbook** + a working **Negotiation Prep Copilot** prompt pack |
| **Career signal** | "Uses AI to drive pipeline velocity and win rate" — directly tied to revenue |

---

## Why This Phase Matters (Market Alignment)

This is where AI touches the P&L. For a business development leader, the durable advantage isn't knowing AI exists — it's operationalizing it into account strategy, qualification, and negotiation. These are the outcomes that show up on a résumé as **revenue impact**, and they map to **VP/SVP Business Development, Chief Commercial Officer, and Growth Strategy** roles.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Build repeatable AI-assisted workflows for account planning and qualification
2. Engineer reliable prompt systems for executive briefings and deal prep
3. Use AI to analyze proposals, pricing trade-offs, and competitive positioning
4. Extract commercial signals from unstructured market/customer data
5. Design human-in-the-loop checkpoints so AI never makes the high-stakes call alone

---

## Week-by-Week Structure

### Week 7 — Prompt Systems for Commercial Work
- **Learn:** [Google Prompting Essentials](https://www.coursera.org/learn/prompting-essentials) — advanced techniques (chaining, meta-prompting)
- **Learn:** [Anthropic Academy](https://anthropic.com/learn) — prompt engineering for real work
- **Do:** Build a reusable prompt template for a "strategic account one-pager"

### Week 8 — AI-Assisted Account Intelligence
- **Learn:** [Wharton — AI for Business](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/wharton-ai-business), *AI in People Management / Marketing* modules (free audit)
- **Do:** Produce AI-drafted account hypotheses for 3 Tier-1 accounts; validate each against what you actually know

### Week 9 — Opportunity Qualification at Scale
- **Do:** Design an AI-supported triage workflow to score and prioritize dormant opportunities (fit, timing, value, access)
- **Do:** Build a bilingual (EN/ES) re-engagement message framework

### Week 10 — Proposal & Pricing Analysis
- **Do:** Use AI to stress-test a proposal: generate objections, competitor counters, and pricing trade-off scenarios
- **Do:** Create a "deal risk checklist" the AI populates from your notes

### Week 11 — Market & Customer Signal Extraction
- **Do:** Summarize industry reports and earnings/news into a customer's strategic priorities (tie to their AI/ESG/cost agenda)
- **Reference:** industrial AI context (McKinsey, [Accenture "From Explore to Ore"](https://www.accenture.com/content/dam/accenture/final/accenture-com/document-3/Accenture-From-Explore-To-Ore.pdf))

### Week 12 — The Negotiation Prep Copilot
- **Do:** Build a prompt pack that prepares you for a negotiation: BATNA analysis, concession ladder, stakeholder map, and objection handling
- **Do:** Add a human-in-the-loop rule set (what AI drafts vs. what only you decide)

### Week 13 — Industry Grounding: AI in Mining & Heavy Industry
- **Learn:** Review verified industry sources (McKinsey mining AI, GlobalData AI-in-mining, Accenture exploration)
- **Do:** Map how your customers' AI-driven cost/safety/sustainability agendas change what they buy — and reframe your value proposition around it

### Week 14 — Capstone Assembly
- **Do:** Assemble the **Strategic Account Playbook** and finalize the **Negotiation Prep Copilot** (see below)

## Capstone Deliverables

### 1. AI-Enhanced Strategic Account Playbook
- Account intelligence template (stakeholders, priorities, risk posture)
- AI-generated hypotheses + human validation section
- Executive-influence map and engagement plan
- Reusable prompt library embedded in the playbook

### 2. Negotiation Prep Copilot (Prompt Pack)
- BATNA / concession-ladder generator
- Objection-and-counter simulator
- Multi-jurisdiction issue flags (commercial/legal/operational)
- Explicit human-in-the-loop guardrails

## Industry Grounding — AI in Mining & Industrial B2B

- ~90% of mining companies have adopted AI in some form, but few have scaled — a first-mover advantage for suppliers who speak the language.
- Commercially relevant themes: predictive maintenance, autonomous operations, mineral targeting/exploration, ESG reporting.
- Use these to frame supplier value against the customer's cost, safety, and sustainability agenda.
