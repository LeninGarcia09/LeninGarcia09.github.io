# Phase 2: Commercial AI for Growth

## Outcomes

1. Increase opportunity quality and speed in strategic pipeline execution
2. Strengthen account planning with AI-supported intelligence
3. Improve negotiation readiness and commercial positioning

## Learning Modules

- AI for strategic account planning and opportunity qualification
- Prompt systems for executive briefings and decision preparation
- AI support in proposal strategy, pricing logic, and trade-off analysis
- Commercial signal extraction from unstructured market data
- Human-in-the-loop decision design for high-stakes deals

## Practical Work

- Launch AI-assisted account planning templates for top strategic accounts
- Build multilingual outreach and reactivation playbooks (English/Spanish)
- Deploy negotiation preparation copilot templates for complex deals

## Target Business Effects

- Better conversion from qualification to negotiation
- Faster preparation cycles for executive and customer meetings
- More consistent commercial narratives across regions

## Industry Grounding — AI in Mining & Industrial B2B

Use cases anchored in his actual market, not generic examples:

- **Where the industry is:** ~90% of mining companies have adopted AI in some form, but few have scaled — creating first-mover commercial advantage for suppliers who speak the language.
- **Commercially relevant AI themes:** predictive maintenance, autonomous operations, mineral targeting/exploration, ESG and sustainability reporting — all shape customer buying priorities.
- **How to use it:** frame supplier value propositions against the customer's AI-driven cost, safety, and sustainability agenda.
