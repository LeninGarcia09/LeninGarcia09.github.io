# First 90 Days: High-Visibility Wins

## Goal

Deliver measurable commercial value while building personal AI leadership capability.

## Days 1-30: Foundation + Diagnostic

### Actions

1. Build an AI opportunity map for top 20 strategic accounts
2. Identify 3 bottlenecks in qualification, proposal, or negotiation cycles
3. Define AI use-case portfolio (quick wins vs strategic bets)
4. Align with CEO and executive team on AI growth thesis and decision rights

### Deliverables

- AI Opportunity Heatmap (by account, region, stage, value)
- AI Use-Case Prioritization Matrix
- Executive one-page strategy narrative

## Days 31-60: Pilot Execution

### Actions

1. Launch 2 pilots:
   - Pilot A: AI-assisted strategic account planning
   - Pilot B: AI-supported negotiation preparation
2. Establish governance checkpoints with legal, IT, and compliance
3. Create prompt and playbook standards for commercial teams

### Deliverables

- Pilot scorecards (baseline vs pilot performance)
- Negotiation prep AI template pack
- Governance checklist for approved commercial AI usage

## Days 61-90: Scale Decision

### Actions

1. Compare pilot impact to baseline
2. Build scale plan for regions/teams with highest readiness
3. Present Board-ready AI value case (benefits, risks, investments, timeline)

### Deliverables

- Scale-or-stop decision memo
- 6-month rollout roadmap
- Board-level AI growth dashboard v1

## 90-Day KPI Targets

| KPI | Baseline | 90-Day Target |
|-----|----------|---------------|
| Time to strategic account brief | Current | -30% |
| Proposal cycle time | Current | -20% |
| Qualified opportunity conversion | Current | +10% |
| Executive confidence in AI initiatives | Qualitative | Strong positive trend |
| Governance compliance on pilot usage | N/A | 100% of approved use cases |
