# Phase 4: Scale and Leadership

## Outcomes

1. Scale AI-enabled commercial practices across regions and teams
2. Operationalize executive governance cadence for sustained value creation
3. Position as enterprise AI growth leader at ELT and Board levels

## Learning Modules

- AI operating model design for global commercial organizations
- Change leadership and adoption in cross-border teams
- Executive communication for AI strategy, risk, and investment
- Portfolio governance for AI initiatives and capability maturity

## Practical Work

- Launch regional rollout plan with adoption checkpoints
- Establish monthly AI growth review with ELT-level metrics
- Build Board update template with decision-ready evidence

## Target Business Effects

- Repeatable AI-enabled commercial execution across markets
- Better strategic focus on high-value opportunities
- Institutional capability rather than one-off individual performance
