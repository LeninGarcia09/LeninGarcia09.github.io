## Why This Phase Matters (Market Alignment)

The difference between "uses AI" and "leads AI transformation" is what separates a candidate from a finalist. This phase builds the leadership artifacts (operating model, board communication, change plan) *and* the personal-brand assets (positioning, LinkedIn, portfolio, interview narrative) that convert your 28-week investment into offers. Target roles: **Chief Commercial Officer, Chief Strategy Officer, VP/SVP Business Development or Growth, and AI Transformation Lead** in industrial sectors.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Design an AI operating model for a global commercial organization
2. Lead adoption and change across cross-border teams
3. Communicate AI strategy, risk, and ROI to executives and boards
4. Govern a portfolio of AI initiatives over time
5. **Position and package** your capability for the executive market

## Week-by-Week Structure

### Week 22 тАФ AI Operating Model Design
- **Learn:** [Wharton тАФ AI for Business](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/wharton-ai-business), *AI Strategy and Governance* capstone module (free audit)
- **Do:** Draft an operating model: roles, decision rights, cadence, and metrics for AI-enabled growth

### Week 23 тАФ Change Leadership & Adoption
- **Learn:** [Microsoft AI Business School](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/) тАФ leadership & culture modules
- **Do:** Build a 90-day adoption plan for one commercial team (training, incentives, quick wins, resistance handling)

### Week 24 тАФ Executive & Board Communication
- **Do:** Create a board-ready AI update template (BLUF format): value delivered, risks managed, decisions needed, next investments
- **Do:** Rehearse a 5-minute AI strategy narrative for a CEO/board audience

### Week 25 тАФ Portfolio Governance & Capability Maturity
- **Do:** Build a simple AI initiative portfolio tracker (value, risk, stage, owner)
- **Do:** Define a capability-maturity model (from ad-hoc тЖТ repeatable тЖТ scaled)

### Week 26 тАФ Career Positioning & Personal Brand
- **Do:** Write your executive positioning statement: *"AI-fluent commercial leader who [specific outcome] in [industry]"*
- **Do:** Rewrite your LinkedIn headline + About section around AI-enabled growth
- **Do:** Identify 15-20 target roles/companies and the keywords they use

### Week 27 тАФ Portfolio Assembly & Proof of Work
- **Do:** Assemble your **Executive AI Career Portfolio** from Phases 1-4 deliverables (Opportunity Thesis, Account Playbook, Clause Library, Operating Model)
- **Do:** Turn each deliverable into a 1-slide "case study" you can walk through in an interview

### Week 28 тАФ Interview Prep & Launch
- **Do:** Prepare STAR stories tying AI work to commercial outcomes
- **Do:** Draft answers to likely executive-interview questions on AI strategy, risk, and ROI
- **Do:** Publish a short thought-leadership post or article demonstrating your point of view
