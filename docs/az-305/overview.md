# AZ-305: Azure Solutions Architect Expert

> **⚠️ This track is under construction.** Challenges coming soon.

## Exam Overview

- **Exam:** AZ-305
- **Level:** Expert (requires AZ-104 prerequisite)
- **Target Audience:** Solutions Architects, Senior Engineers
- **Cost:** $165 USD

## Why This Exam Matters for AI Architects

AZ-305 validates the infrastructure and governance foundation that every AI solution depends on. AI agents need networking (private endpoints, VNet), storage (ADLS Gen2), identity (Entra ID, managed identities), and governance (Azure Policy, Blueprints) — all AZ-305 domains.

## Skills Measured

|--------|--------|

| Design data storage solutions | 20-25% |
